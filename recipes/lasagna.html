Directions to Prepare:
  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Preheat the oven
  5. Assemble the lasagna: first layer
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of large baking dish
    2. Sprinkle a generous layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top
  6. Repeat previous step for every layer wanted
  7. Cover and bake
  8. Remove foil and finish baking
  9. Rest and serve
